## 📧 Optional: Get Email Notifications

To receive email notifications when someone submits the form:

1. Go to https://forms.google.com
2. Find your "Gruha Constructions Inquiry" form
3. Click the **⋮** (three dots) → **Settings**
4. Scroll to "Notifications"
5. Check **"Email notifications for new responses"**
6. Click **Save**

Now you'll get emailed every time someone submits!
